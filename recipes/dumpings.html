Dumpings

Description

These Chinese dumplings are from Liana Cafe House in Seattle's International District. They sell these traditional fried pork dumplings from a tiny takeout shop but with this recipe, you can see how to make them at home!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Large egg
  • Finely chopped Chinese chives
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Minced fresh ginger
  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

How to cook

  1. Prepare dumplings: Mix beef, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  2. Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon about 1 tablespoon of the filling in the middle. Wet the edge with a little water and crimp together forming small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat to form remaining dumplings
  3. Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes per side. Pour in 1 cup of water; cover and cook until the dumplings are tender and pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
